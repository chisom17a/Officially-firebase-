Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2025

At Chiearn Hub, your privacy and trust are extremely important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, process, use, and secure the information we receive from you.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect information you provide, including your full name, email address, phone number, payment details, usage details, and activity logs. We may also collect device information, IP address, and browser metadata.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data allows us to create and manage your account, process transactions, improve platform safety, deliver customer support, and personalize your user experience.

3. Data Protection & Security

We use encryption, secure storage systems, and continuous monitoring to protect your information. Your data is never stored in plain text and is handled under strict confidentiality policies.

4. Third-Party Sharing

Your information is never sold. It is only shared with trusted partners for payment processing, identity verification, fraud prevention, or to comply with legal obligations.

5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Cookies help us improve load speeds, personalize activity, and analyze performance. You may disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

6. GDPR / CCPA Compliance

Depending on your location, you retain the right to access, delete, restrict, or export your information. We support data transparency and user privacy rights globally.

7. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify users in the event of major changes. Continued use of the platform means you accept the updated terms.
